LET General Education Coverage

1. English

Study and Thinking Skills: This topic covers the development of critical thinking, reading, and analytical skills. It focuses on techniques for effective learning and problem-solving.

Writing in the Discipline: This topic emphasizes writing skills tailored to specific academic disciplines. It involves understanding and practicing the conventions of academic writing in various fields.

Speech and Oral Communication: This area explores effective verbal communication, including public speaking, interpersonal communication, and presentation skills.

Philippine Literature: This subject delves into the rich literary heritage of the Philippines, encompassing both classic and contemporary works.

Master Works of the World: This topic involves the study of significant literary works from various cultures and time periods, fostering an appreciation for global literary masterpieces.

2. Filipino

Komunikasyon sa Akademikong Filipino: This topic focuses on effective communication in academic settings using the Filipino language.

Pagbasa at Pagsulat tungo sa Pananaliksik: It covers reading and writing skills geared towards research and academic inquiry.

Masining na Pagpapahayag: This area emphasizes artistic and creative expression in Filipino, including poetry, drama, and other forms of literary artistry.

3. Mathematics

Fundamentals of Math: This covers essential mathematical concepts and principles, including arithmetic, basic algebra, and problem-solving techniques.

Plane Geometry: This topic deals with the properties and relationships of geometric figures in two dimensions.

Elementary Algebra: It extends algebraic knowledge to more complex topics and problem-solving.

Statistics and Probability: This area focuses on data analysis, probability theory, and statistical methods.

4. Science

Biological Science - General Biology: This encompasses the study of living organisms, their structures, functions, and ecosystems.

Physical Science - with Earth Science: This includes topics in physics, chemistry, and earth sciences, providing a comprehensive understanding of the physical world.

5. Social Sciences

Philippine Government New Constitution with Human Rights: This area covers the political structure of the Philippines, the constitution, and human rights issues.

Philippine History: It explores the historical events and developments of the Philippines.

Basic Economics, Taxation, Agrarian Reform: This involves understanding economic principles, taxation systems, and agrarian reform policies.

Society, Culture with Family Planning: It delves into societal norms, cultural practices, and family planning strategies.

Rizal and Other Heroes: This topic discusses the life and contributions of Jose Rizal and other Philippine national heroes.

Philosophy of Man: It explores philosophical concepts related to human nature and existence.

Arts: This covers various forms of art, including visual arts, music, and performing arts.

General Psychology: This area examines fundamental psychological concepts and principles.

Information and Communication Technology: It involves understanding technology and its role in communication and society.
