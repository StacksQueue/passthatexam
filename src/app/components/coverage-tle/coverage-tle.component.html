LET Agriculture and Fishery Arts Coverage

1. Basic Drafting

This topic introduces the fundamentals of technical drawing and drafting techniques. It includes instruction on creating and interpreting technical drawings, blueprints, and schematics used in various technical and engineering fields.

2. Business Math

Business math focuses on mathematical concepts and skills used in practical business and financial applications. It covers topics such as percentages, interest, profit and loss, discounts, and financial calculations for business transactions.

3. Basic Electricity

Basic electricity explores the principles of electrical circuits, components, and electrical safety. It includes topics such as electrical currents, voltage, resistance, and circuit analysis.

4. Basic Plumbing

This area covers the fundamentals of plumbing systems, including the installation, maintenance, and repair of pipes, fixtures, and plumbing equipment. It also emphasizes proper plumbing codes and regulations.

5. Cosmetology

Cosmetology encompasses the study of beauty treatments, hair care, skincare, and cosmetics. It includes topics like hairstyling, makeup application, skin care, and salon management.

6. Foods

Foods education focuses on culinary skills and techniques, including food preparation, cooking methods, menu planning, and nutrition. It also covers food safety and sanitation practices.

7. Carpentry and Masonry

Carpentry and masonry teach the skills needed for construction and woodworking projects. Topics include woodworking tools, building materials, construction techniques, and safety protocols.

8. Basic Electronics

Basic electronics introduces the principles of electronic circuits, components, and devices. It covers topics such as diodes, transistors, circuits, and electronic troubleshooting.

9. Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship education provides knowledge and skills related to starting and managing a business. It includes business planning, marketing strategies, financial management, and entrepreneurship ethics.
