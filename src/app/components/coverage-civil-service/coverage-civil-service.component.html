Civil Service Exam Coverage

1. Career Service Professional Examination (CSE-PPT)

The CSE-PPT is the higher-level exam and is intended for individuals who are seeking professional-level positions in the government. It covers the following areas:

General Ability Test (GAT):

Verbal Reasoning: Vocabulary, grammar, paragraph organization, reading comprehension

Analytical Ability: Data interpretation, logic, and numerical reasoning

Specialized Professional Knowledge (SPECK):

This section assesses the specialized knowledge and skills required for specific positions. It varies depending on the field or sector in which the candidate intends to work.

2. Career Service Subprofessional Examination (CSE-SPE)

The CSE-SPE is for individuals who are interested in subprofessional positions in the government. It covers the following areas:

General Ability Test (GAT):

Verbal Reasoning: Vocabulary, grammar, paragraph organization, reading comprehension

Analytical Ability: Data interpretation, logic, and numerical reasoning

Subprofessional (Subprof) Knowledge (SUBPROF):

This section assesses the knowledge and skills relevant to subprofessional positions, such as clerical or technical roles.
