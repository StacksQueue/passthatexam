LET Values Education Coverage

1. Foundation of Values Education

Philosophical and Ethical Foundations of VE: This topic explores the philosophical and ethical principles that underpin values education, examining various moral and ethical theories.

Socio-Cultural, Anthropological & Legal Bases of VE: Discusses the societal, cultural, and legal influences on values education and their role in shaping individual and collective values.

2. Psychological Theories of Values Development

Examines psychological theories that explain how values are developed and shaped in individuals, including stages of moral development and psychological factors influencing value formation.

3. Personhood Development

Personhood Development: Focuses on the holistic development of individuals, including their cognitive, emotional, social, and moral dimensions.

Dynamics of Intra and Interpersonal Relations: Explores how individuals interact with themselves and others, emphasizing healthy relationships and communication.

Psycho-Spiritual Development: Addresses the spiritual and emotional aspects of personal growth and values development.

Management of Behavior Problems: Provides strategies for addressing and managing behavior problems in educational settings.

4. Transformative Education (Sources of Values and Factors in Values Education)

Family Life and Filipino Values System: Examines the role of family and the Filipino cultural context in shaping values.

Education for Human Rights and Responsible Citizenship: Focuses on the promotion of human rights, responsible citizenship, and social justice.

Foundations of Gender Studies: Explores issues related to gender equality and diversity.

Peace and Global Education: Addresses the importance of peace education and global perspectives in values education.

Information Technology and Human Development: Discusses the impact of technology on values and human development.

Moral Issues and Concerns in Contemporary Living: Examines contemporary moral issues and ethical dilemmas.

5. Work Ethics and Community Service: Commitment to Social Responsibility and Accountability

Career Development and Work Values: Covers work ethics, values in the workplace, and career development.

Values Education through Community Service: Discusses the role of community service in values education.

6. Approaches and Methodologies

Facilitation: Theory and Practice: Explores facilitation techniques for values education.

Teaching Approaches and Strategies in Transformative Education: Covers pedagogical methods for teaching values effectively.

Values Integration in the School Setting: Addresses how values can be integrated into the school curriculum and culture.

7. Research and Evaluation

Behavioral Statistics: Covers statistical methods used in behavioral research.

Development of VE Instruction Materials and Assessment Tools: Discusses the design and development of instructional materials and assessments for values education.

Research in VE: Explores research methodologies and practices in values education.
