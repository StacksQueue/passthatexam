LET MAPEH Coverage

1. Foundations of MAPEH

This topic provides an introduction to the fundamental principles and concepts that underpin the MAPEH curriculum, including its historical, philosophical, and educational foundations.

2. Methods and Strategies of Teaching MAPEH

Discusses pedagogical approaches and instructional strategies specific to teaching music, arts, physical education, and health effectively in the classroom.

3. Coaching and Officiating of Sports Events, Dance Competitions, and Music Activities

Covers the skills and knowledge needed to coach and officiate sports events, manage dance competitions, and lead music activities, including understanding the rules, techniques, and logistics involved.

4. Organization and Management

Explores the administrative and organizational aspects of MAPEH programs, including curriculum planning, resource management, and co-curricular activities.

5. Research

Focuses on research methodologies and practices in the field of MAPEH, emphasizing the importance of evidence-based decision-making and curriculum development.

6. Special Education in MAPEH

Addresses inclusive education and strategies for teaching MAPEH to students with special needs, promoting equitable access to physical education and the arts.

7. Physical Education

Anatomical, Mechanical, and Physiological Bases of Movement: Examines the scientific foundations of human movement, including the anatomy, mechanics, and physiology of the human body.

Gymnastics: Covers various forms of gymnastics, including Philippine folk dance, international folk dance, and other dance forms, as well as the principles of artistic and rhythmic gymnastics.

Team Sports: Focuses on team sports such as basketball, volleyball, and soccer, including rules, techniques, and coaching strategies.

Aquatics: Addresses swimming and water-related activities, emphasizing water safety and swimming skills.

Athletics, Individual, Dual, and Combative Sports: Explores individual and combative sports, including track and field, wrestling, and martial arts.

8. Health Education

Personal, Community, Environmental Health: Covers topics related to personal health, community health promotion, and environmental health issues.

Safety Education and First Aid: Addresses safety education, emergency response, and basic first aid techniques.

Health Practicum Provides practical experiences in health education, allowing candidates to apply their knowledge in real-world settings.

9. Music

Philippine and Asian Music: Explores the musical traditions of the Philippines and Asia.

Solfeggio and Applied Piano: Covers solfeggio (ear training) and piano skills.

Integrated Music Theory: Focuses on music theory, including notation, scales, and harmony.

Music Literature: Examines music history and repertoire.

Choral Works and Conducting: Addresses choral music, conducting techniques, and ensemble performance.

Rondalla Playing and Instrumentation: Covers rondalla ensemble music and musical instrument techniques.

10. Art Education

This topic explores the theory and practice of visual arts education, including principles of design, art history, and hands-on artistic expression.
