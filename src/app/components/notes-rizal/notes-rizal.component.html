4. Other Works

  1. Sa Aking Mga Kabata: First poem written by Rizal.
  2. Mi Ultimo Adios (Ang Huling Paalam): The best poem ever written by Dr. Jose Rizal.
  3. La Indolencia de los Filipinos (The Indolence of Filipinos): The book written by Rizal in defense of the alleged laziness of the Filipinos.
  4. Laon Laan / Laong Laan: Pen name used by Rizal in his writings in La Solidaridad.
  5. Pahimakas: A translation of Rizal's farewell poem written by Andres Bonifacio in Tagalog.
  6. Feb 15, 1889: Date of founding of the fortnightly newspaper "La Solidaridad."
  7. Nov. 15, 1895: Date of the last issue of La Solidaridad.
  8. RA 1425 / Rizal Law: The law which provides that "courses on the life, works and writings of Jose Rizal, particularly his novels Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo, shall be included in the curricula of all schools, colleges, and universities, public or private."
  9. Draco Rizali, Rachphorous Rizali, Apogonia Rizali: Rizal discovered 3 species named after him: Draco Rizali (Wandolleck), a species of flying dragon, Rachphorous Rizali (Boetger), a hitherto unknown species of toad, Apogonia Rizali (Heller), a small beetle.
