LET Agriculture and Fishery Arts Coverage

1. Functional Application of Knowledge

This topic encompasses the practical application of agricultural and fishery knowledge and skills. It involves the ability to apply scientific principles to real-world scenarios in farming and fisheries.

2. Breeds of Farm Animals and Fish

Covers the identification, characteristics, and management of various breeds of farm animals (such as cattle, poultry, and swine) and fish species (such as tilapia, catfish, and trout). Understanding breed characteristics is essential for optimizing production and resource management.

3. Pests and Diseases Affecting Animals and Fish

Addresses the identification, prevention, and control of common pests and diseases that affect farm animals and fish. Knowledge in this area is crucial for maintaining healthy livestock and fish populations.

4. Propagation

Focuses on the techniques and methods for breeding and propagating farm animals and fish. This includes topics like artificial insemination, selective breeding, and hatchery management.

5. Marketing Strategies in the Industry/Entrepreneurship

Explores marketing and business strategies in the agriculture and fishery industry. This includes understanding market dynamics, value chain analysis, entrepreneurship skills, and business planning for agricultural and fishery enterprises.

6. Proper Care and Management of Agricultural and Fishery Products

Addresses post-harvest practices, handling, and storage of agricultural and fishery products. It includes techniques to ensure product quality, reduce spoilage, and maintain the integrity of products from farm to market.
